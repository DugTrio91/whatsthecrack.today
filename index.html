Need Advice?

Springboard is one of the largest and longest established training providers in the North East of England. Over the past 40 years we have grown into a vibrant organisation with excellent premises and facilities.

At Springboard we believe everyone has the ability and the right to learn and develop themselves. We make learning fun and enjoyable and our aim is to help people work towards a brighter future.

If you are unemployed, over 19 years of age and need help finding or preparing for work, then our Springboard Works programme can help you.

Springboard can support you in various vocational areas, including:


  • Childcare
  • Elderely Care
  • Business Admin
  • Customer Service
  • Construction
  • and many more...
